Reliable dog walking and pet care

At Walks & Whiskers, we pride ourselves on providing you with a friendly, reliable and professional dog walking service. Treating each dog as if they were our own, we enjoy giving them the love, care and attention they need and deserve on their walks.

We appreciate that in today's busy world it's not easy spending as much time as you would like with your pet. Whether it's walking your dog whilst you are at work or busy minding a new baby or looking after your smaller pets when you are away from home on holiday or business; Walks & Whiskers is here to help.

Dog Walking

All of our walks include a maximum of four dogs and discounted rates are available for multiple dogs from the same household.

Please note that in the event of a cancellation 24hrs notice is required; otherwise a fee will be applicable. With holiday bookings for small pets and horses we require a 50% deposit payable upon booking and 1 weeks notice in the event of a cancellation; otherwise a fee will be applicable.

All Bank Holidays, Christmas Day, Boxing Day and New Years Day are charged at double price.
